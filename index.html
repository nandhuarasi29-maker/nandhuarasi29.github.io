My Journey

I began my career as a **Software Engineer**, writing code and developing a deep appreciation for execution, database optimization, and clear API routing. This technical foundation gave me the ability to view complex systems from the inside out.

Driven by a curiosity to understand how software serves complex business operations, I transitioned into **Business Analysis** within the Banking and Financial Services (BFSI) domain. This allowed me to bridge the technical divide—translating complicated compliance parameters and transaction checks into precise developer blueprints.

Today, I act as a **Technical Product Owner** and Lead Business Analyst, defining roadmap strategies and owning delivery lifecycles for real-time risk platforms. I combine my coding background, a business-centric mindset, and a passion for technology to help build secure, robust applications.
